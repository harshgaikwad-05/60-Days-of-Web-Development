BMW M8 Competition

The BMW M8 Competition is BMW’s flagship performance 8 Series, combining supercar-level speed with luxury grand touring comfort. Powered by a 617-hp twin-turbo V8, it rockets from 0–60 mph in about 3 seconds and comes in Coupe, Convertible, and Gran Coupe forms, all featuring M xDrive all-wheel drive and track-focused engineering.
